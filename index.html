Powerful animation framework

  • PwrSlide ships with over 50 animations from Animate.css
  • PwrSlides and PwrPieces descend from the PwrAnimated Object
  • Wrap any element in <pwr-piece> tags for them to become animated
    • <pwr-piece> tags are animated by the slide in descending order
      •
    • Add the 'show' attribute to the <pwr-piece> tag to show its contents initially
